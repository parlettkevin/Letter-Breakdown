## Anti-Patterns Verdict

Not AI slop. Zero gradient text, zero side-stripe accent borders, zero glassmorphism, zero decorative shadows. Amber three-tier palette is deliberate. Detector found 2 warnings: single-font (Hanken Grotesk only — partial false positive for utility context) and flat-type-hierarchy (11–18px in 7 steps, too compressed — real finding). No browser overlay attempted.

## Overall Impression

Thoughtful, well-made field tool by someone who uses it. iOS PDF delivery, autofill suppression, safe-area inset — the real-device details are correct. Main gap: the form is ordered like a mortgage application, not a phone conversation. The monthly payment total is buried in a list that looks like everything else around it. Biggest opportunity: make the answer visually unavoidable.

## What's Working

1. Sticky Calculate bar with safe-area inset — always in thumb zone, correct disable/re-enable behavior
2. iOS PDF delivery via Web Share API with user-agent sniff + canShare check + anchor fallback — correctly implemented
3. Input UX tuned for live number entry — inputmode, autocomplete:off, comma formatting, autofill color suppression

## Priority Issues

**[P0] Cards span wall-to-wall on a real phone**
- `.dml-root{padding:0 0 24px}` — zero horizontal margin. On 375px screen, rounded-corner cards clip against viewport edge.
- Fix: `padding: 0 12px 24px` on `.dml-root`. Sticky bar needs `margin: 0 -12px` to stay full-width.
- Suggested command: /impeccable polish

**[P1] Monthly payment total not visually dominant**
- Total renders at 18px #b05a0a with only a 1px amber border-top — indistinguishable at glance from the 4 line-items above it. Flat type hierarchy finding from detector agrees.
- Fix: 26–28px for total value, more vertical spacing above it. Optionally a labeled "Total Monthly" header.
- Suggested command: /impeccable bolder

**[P1] Closing Costs card too dense for a 5-minute call**
- 10+ interaction targets plus two paragraphs of policy text in one card.
- Fix: Collapse discount points, origination credit, and title insurance into a `<details>` "Advanced closing costs" — collapsed by default.
- Suggested command: /impeccable distill

**[P2] No "New Quote" reset path**
- Session save covers loan type, down%, dates — but not price, rate, or borrower name. Running a second back-to-back quote means 6–8 manual field clears.
- Fix: Ghost "New quote" button/link that clears price, rate, name, address, resets results, preserves persistent defaults.
- Suggested command: /impeccable harden

**[P2] Type hierarchy compressed in the critical reading zone**
- 7 size steps from 11–18px; the 11–16px band has 6 sizes in a 5px range. Section headers at 12px read smaller than labels at 13px.
- Fix: Collapse to 4 intentional sizes: 11px (.dml-small only), 13px (labels), 16px (result values), 22–26px (totals).
- Suggested command: /impeccable typeset

## Persona Red Flags

**Casey (Distracted Mobile):** Rate field ~100px wide in 3-up row — smallest tap target for most critical input. PDF and Copy buttons share same card with no visual separation. `.dml-small` at 11px #706b60 unreadable in sunlight. No horizontal root padding means near-edge taps miss fields.

**Riley (Stress Tester):** Pasting "400,000" from Zillow triggers parseInt stopping at comma — silently produces $400 loan. 0% down on FHA/Conventional generates no warning. Refresh loses price, rate, and borrower name (not included in saveSession).

**Kevin the Loan Officer:** Tab order hits name/address before price/rate — 7+ tabs to reach rate from page load. No scroll-to-results after Calculate — results appear in-place with no motion cue. Download Options card appears FIRST in results before the monthly payment answer.
